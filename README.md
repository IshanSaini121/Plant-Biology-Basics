# Amazing Plants

Welcome to the **World of Amazing Plants**

This project have a beginner-friendly repository for understanding the fundamentals of plant biology. This includes topics such as plant anatomy, physiology, genetics, ecology, taxonomy and evolution. Useful for students, educators and enthusiasts looking for concise notes and resources on plant science. 

## Content

- Lesson Plans and Worksheets
- Basics of all Plant Groups
- Fun Plant Facts
- Simple Experiments and Activites for Studetns
- Plant Care Guide

## Why Study Plants?

Plants are the foundation of life on Earth. They provide oxygen, food, medicine, shelter and raw materials. Studying plants helps us understand ecosystems, improve agriculture, discover new medicines and address global challenges like climate change, biodiversity loss and food security.

**Let's Explore fascinating World of Plants Together!**

## Sample Activity: Seed Germination

1. Take two sets of seeds (e.g., beans or mustard) and place one set on moist cotton/soil and the other on dry cotton/soil.
2. Keep them under sunlight and water only the moist set regularly.
3. Observe daily changes like swelling, root emergence, and shoot growth.
4. Compare both sets to conclude that water, air, and suitable conditions are essential for seed germination.
